## 🏭 Featured Enterprise Products

### 🏭 REM — Robust Enterprise Management (ERP)
A modern, scalable enterprise resource planning system engineered for high-throughput corporate operations.
* **Core Modules:** Human Resources • Payroll Management • Finance & Accounting • Procurement Logistics • Inventory Control • CRM & Real-Time Reporting Analytics.
* **Infrastructure:** High-availability architecture utilizing Node.js with complex relational schemas hosted via Cloud PostgreSQL (Neon) and Render.

### 🌍 eGov Digital Services Platform
A secure, national-grade GovTech solution designed to digitize public administration workflows and citizen interactions.
* **Key Features:** Secure Citizen Profiles • Automated Document Requests & Issuance (Birth/Marriage certificates) • Cryptographic Form Processing • Administrative Multi-tenant Portals.

### 🎓 Robust School Management System
A comprehensive, multi-tenant academic and financial tracking platform for educational institutions.
* **Key Features:** Student & Teacher Lifecycle Management • Real-Time Tuition Reconciliation Widgets • Attendance Tracking & Academic Records.
